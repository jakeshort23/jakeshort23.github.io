Who Are The Mennonites?

A small Christian denomination in existence for 500 years, Mennonites remain largerly unknown or seen through a series of stereotypes (some true, some not). Yes, we are related to the Amish. Here you will find much more about our history, our beliefs, our culture, our struggles, and beyond.

Style & Fashion

What I wear on a daily basis expresses who I am, what I am feeling, and what I am doing. Some days my clothes are chosen for a specific purpose. Some days I throw on just what is clean and ironed. But every day I try to look my best. My closet represents a journey, so come along for the ride.

D.C. Living

One thing Mennonites and Washington, D.C. have in common is being stereotyped in many ways. Life in D.C. is constantly changing, even moreso in recent years. Follow along as I document my daily life and discover different neighborhoods in the city I've come to call my home.
